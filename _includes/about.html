  • Since 1956

    Our Beginnings

    SKKN formed in 1956 to promote Indian Arts and Culture, SKKN was Very Active in 1950s through 1980s, Staged more than 250 drama productions in Singapore and several Variety shows; Was in hibernation for more than a decade.

  • 2016

    Relaunched SKKN

    Relaunched Singapore Kairalee Kala Nilayam in 2016 with new members during Pravasi Express Nite. The function was followed by musical treat led by G Venugopal and drama - Kannadi by Singapore Kiralee Kala Nilayam Team.
